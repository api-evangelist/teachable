A guide explaining that a learning management system (LMS) is software for creating, delivering, tracking, and reporting on training programs. It emphasizes how modern platforms differ from legacy systems by serving distributed and deskless workforces through mobile accessibility, bulk enrollment without corporate email requirements, and location-level reporting rather than per-seat pricing.
